Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

The purpose of our Zen garden design is to provide users with a fun, natural feel to their Zen garden experience. Our theme is “campsite”. Users will notice earth-tones in our color choices and feel the peace that so often accompanies a weekend-getaway to the woods.
